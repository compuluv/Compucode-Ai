How It Works

AI doesn't replace your staff. It covers the gaps when calls go unanswered.

1. Your team gets first chance

Your phone rings normally. If someone answers, business continues exactly as usual.

Normal workflow No disruption

2. AI steps in only when needed

If nobody answers, the AI Backup Receptionist greets the caller, gathers details, and keeps the lead engaged.

Overflow support Lead retention

3. You get the lead instantly

The call summary, callback request, or booking intent is sent to your team so someone can follow up quickly.
