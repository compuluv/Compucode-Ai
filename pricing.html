Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a retainer plan with my website? +
Not required, but recommended. Without a plan, updates, hosting issues, and changes are billed hourly. The Basic plan at $97/mo covers your ongoing maintenance and is the most cost-effective option for most clients.
How does the AI Voice Receptionist work? +
We build a custom AI phone agent trained on your business — your hours, services, pricing, FAQs, and booking flow. It answers calls in your brand's voice 24/7. You receive summaries and leads by email or SMS. Setup takes 5–10 business days.
What's included in the "monthly plan" for AI systems? +
Your Grow ($297) or Command ($597) retainer plan covers hosting, monitoring, prompt updates, and performance reporting for your AI systems. The Basic plan does not include AI system management.
Do prices include taxes? +
All listed prices are in Canadian dollars (CAD) before applicable taxes. HST will be added for Ontario-based clients. A formal quote with all tax details is provided before any work begins.
How do I get started? +
Email us at hello@compucodeai.com or call our AI Receptionist demo line. We'll scope your project, send a fixed-price quote within 24 hours, and get started once you've approved it. No contracts — just results.
Can I see examples of your work? +
Absolutely. Recent builds include dadsauto.ca (automotive repair), superiorwatch.co (luxury watch service), and danforthbarber.ca (barbershop). Each is a custom-coded, fully responsive site with SEO and structured data — no templates.
